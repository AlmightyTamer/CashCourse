About CashCourse

We believe financial literacy should be free, accessible, and practical. CashCourse provides comprehensive financial education for everyone, no matter where they start.

The Team

Built by two people who wanted better financial education.

Aditiya Navin
★ Co-Founder

Aditiya is passionate about making financial knowledge accessible to everyone. He co-founded CashCourse to bridge the gap between complex financial concepts and everyday decision-making.

Param Tyagi
★ Co-Founder & Lead Developer

Param leads the technical vision behind CashCourse, building the entire platform as a fast, free, single-file web app. He believes that great financial education should feel as good as it teaches.

Our Mission

Financial literacy is one of the most powerful skills a person can have — yet it's rarely taught well. CashCourse exists to change that: nine chapters, 63 lessons, completely free, forever. No ads, no paywalls, no gatekeeping.
