Our Story

We believe financial education changes lives.

CashCourse was born from a simple frustration: why is personal finance taught so poorly in schools? We set out to fix that.

Inspired by the best in education

When Khan Academy proved that world-class education could be free for anyone with an internet connection, it changed everything. We asked: why isn't there a Khan Academy for personal finance?

CashCourse brings that same philosophy — short, visual, self-paced lessons that meet learners where they are — to the topics that matter most in everyday life.

No textbook jargon. No paywalls. Just clear, actionable knowledge you can use today.

"Financial literacy is not a luxury. It's the foundation of every other life decision."
— CashCourse founding team
