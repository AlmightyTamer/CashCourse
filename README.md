Inspired by platforms like Khan Academy, MoneyMap breaks down complex financial concepts into simple videos, explanations, and interactive exercises. ## Mission Our goal is to make financial knowledge accessible to everyone by teaching the skills people need to manage money, build wealth, and make informed financial decisions. ## What You'll Learn MoneyMap covers a wide range of personal finance topics, including: - Budgeting and expense tracking - Saving and emergency funds - Credit scores and debt management - Investing fundamentals - Taxes and real-world financial decisions - Behavioral finance and money psychology ## Structure The curriculum is organized into short, focused modules: 1. **Foundations of Money** – Understanding money, banks, and inflation 2. **Budgeting & Saving** – Managing income and expenses 3. **Credit & Debt** – Loans, credit scores, and interest 4. **Investing Basics** – Stocks, bonds, ETFs, and compound growth 5. **Real‑World Finance** – Buying a car, renting vs buying, insurance 6. **Advanced Concepts** – Financial planning and long‑term wealth building ## Goals of the Project - Make financial education free and accessible - Create clear, visual learning content - Provide practical real‑world financial skills - Build a structured curriculum anyone can follow
